<h3>📉 Handling Expenses During Construction</h3>

Handling cost entries while `Under Construction`:

1. Direct Costs: The price of the RV and materials for construction are added to your asset's cost basis.
2. Indirect Costs: Items like insurance or storage fees paid before the RV is ready for rent must generally be capitalized into the cost of the RV.
3. Depreciation: You cannot start taking depreciation (the Section 179 deduction or regular MACRS) until the year the RV is actually available for rent.
